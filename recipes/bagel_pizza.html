Bagel Pizza

Description

Bagel pizzas are simple to make and take no time at all. Each one can be customized so everyone will be happy with their individual pizzas.

Ingredients

  • Bagels
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Spinach
  • Basil
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Slice the bagels in half and toast in the oven.
  2. Spread marinara sauce on each bagel slice.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the marinara sauce.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients as toppings, as desired.
  5. Pop in the oven until the cheese melts and browns slightly.
