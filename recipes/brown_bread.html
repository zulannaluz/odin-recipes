Irish Brown Bread

Description

I learned how to make this bread in Galway, Ireland. I have no idea how authentic it is but I consider it Irish.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • White flour
  • Whole wheat flour
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Pour the milk, water, and oil into the bread machine container.
  2. Add the salt, sugar, and both kind of flour as well.
  3. Shake the container my grabbing the metal handle and twisting.
  4. Sprinkle a measure of yeast over the mix.
  5. Pop the container into the bread machine for four hours.
