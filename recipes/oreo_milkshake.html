Oreo Milkshake

Description

One of my favorite desserts, this can be found at many fast food restaurants but can also be made at home.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Oreo cookies
  • Chocolate sauce
  • Cherries

Steps

  1. Add a generous helping of vanilla ice cream to a blender.
  2. Add a handful of oreo cookies over that.
  3. Blend until you have a smooth texture.
  4. Serve in a chilled glass. Drizzle chocolate sauce over that and add a cherry on top.
