Guatemalan Rellenitos

Ingredients

  • 5 Ripe plantains
  • A small can black beans
  • A couple tablespoons of sugar
  • A couple tablespoons of cinnammon
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash the plantains.
  2. Cut them into 3 inch sections without removing the peels.
  3. Steam the plantains until the flesh is soft.
  4. Put olive oil in a small frying pan and heat on high.
  5. Remove the peels and plantain stems.
  6. Mash the plantain flesh until smooth.
  7. Heat the beans and mix in a generous amount of cinnammon, to taste.
  8. Let plantains and beans cool until they are comfortable to handle.
  9. Shape a spoonful of mashed plantain into a flat round shape.
  10. Add a dollop of beans to the center. Encase the beans with the plantain mash.
  11. Drop the rolls into the hot frying pan and turn frequently.
  12. Place the fried plantain rolls on a paper towel.
  13. Sprinkle with a generous amount of sugar while hot.
  14. Enjoy!
